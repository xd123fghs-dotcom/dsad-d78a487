Five Nights at Freddy's Survival Horror

Can you survive until 6 AM? Follow these core tactics to stay alive inside the security office:

  • Watch the Doors: Check the lights constantly. Do not close the doors unless you see an animatronic standing right there. Saving power is your key to survival.
  • Listen Carefully: Audio cues are everything. Heavy footsteps mean Bonnie or Chica are moving close to your hallways. Freddy's laugh means he is progressing forward.
  • Monitor Foxy: Check Pirate Cove regularly on your camera to keep Foxy stalled. If he disappears, close your left door immediately before he sprints into your room!
Siren Head: Legacy Action / Sandbox

People think this game is terrifying, but let's be honest—players bully Siren Head here every single day! Here is how to play:

  • The "Scary" Truth: Do not be afraid! Siren Head is basically a punching bag for experienced players. He gets chased down and trolled 24/7.
  • Use the Environment: Use trees, rocks, and buildings to break his line of sight if he tries to chase you. Vehicles are also great for speeding away or circling around him.
  • Stick with the Squad: Gather a group of players. It is much easier (and hilariously fun) to distract him, run in circles, and juke his attacks together.
